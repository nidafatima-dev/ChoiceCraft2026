## Rock-Paper-Scissors

A simple interactive game where you play Rock, Paper, Scissors against the computer.

### Features:
- Click on Stone, Paper, or Scissors to play.
- Score tracking for user and computer.
- Responsive and interactive design.
- Smooth animations and modern UI.

### How to Play:
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click on your choice: Stone 🪨, Paper 📄, or Scissors ✂️.
3. See the result and watch your score update.
4. Click Reset to start over.
